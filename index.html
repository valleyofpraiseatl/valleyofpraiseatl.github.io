Our Vision

To uplift people’s souls through praise and worship in a way that honors God and creates positive impact.

Our Mission

Valley of Praise Corp is an IRS approved 501(c)3 non-profit organization with a mission to promote social standards and to instill Christian’s values in children and youths’ life; to coach and train young people in different capacities of confidence, community service, and the music arts with a goal of building a peaceful community.

Purposes

  • Religious, Charitable, and Educational
  • Building a peaceful and joyful community
  • Serving our community
  • Declaring praise and singing gospel music to glorify God and to edify the community
  • Reducing stress, anxiety, and contributing to overall mental and emotional health and wellbeing through word and song
  • Making people feel valued and increasing their confidence/self-esteem

Goals

  • Be available and welcoming
  • Learn from each other and share knowledge/information
  • Become a comforter that others can count on
  • Relax and socialize

Motivation

  • Biblical Values
  • Confidence: Building up Self-esteem
  • Carrying out Arts
  • Community Service
  • Learning Media

Activities

  • Teaching Biblical Verses -Verses Challenge
  • Exploring Instrument Talent
  • Vocalization and Singing
  • Dancing/Choreography
  • Community Services
  • Socialization/Games
